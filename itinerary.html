Paris - 7 Days

France, Europe

📅 7 Days | $1,200 - $1,800

View Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Montmartre

Arrive in Paris, settle into your hotel. Visit the charming Montmartre district, explore the Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoy street art.

Day 2: Eiffel Tower & Seine River

Climb the Eiffel Tower for panoramic views. Take a romantic Seine river cruise in the evening and dine at a riverside restaurant.

Day 3: Louvre & Tuileries

Explore the world-famous Louvre Museum. Stroll through the Tuileries Garden and visit Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 4: Versailles Palace

Day trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles. Walk through the gorgeous gardens and Hall of Mirrors.

Day 5: Arc de Triomphe & Champs-Élysées

Visit the Arc de Triomphe, walk down the famous Champs-Élysées for shopping and dining.

Day 6: Latin Quarter & Marais

Explore the vibrant Latin Quarter with cafes and bookstores. Visit the trendy Marais district with boutiques and museums.

Day 7: Leisure & Departure

Relax at a local café, do last-minute shopping, or revisit your favorite spots before departure.

Tokyo - 7 Days

Japan, Asia

📅 7 Days | $1,500 - $2,200

View Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Shibuya

Arrive in Tokyo. Visit the famous Shibuya Crossing, explore the bustling Shibuya district and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Asakusa & Skytree

Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa. Climb Tokyo Skytree for amazing city views. Browse the traditional shopping street.

Day 3: Meiji Shrine & Harajuku

Walk through the peaceful Meiji Shrine forest. Explore the quirky fashion and youth culture of Harajuku district.

Day 4: Tsukiji Market & Ginza

Experience the vibrant Tsukiji Outer Market. Shop at luxury boutiques in Ginza and enjoy fine dining.

Day 5: Imperial Palace & Marunouchi

Tour the beautiful Imperial Palace East Gardens. Explore the modern business district of Marunouchi.

Day 6: Akihabara & Shinjuku

Visit anime and electronics shops in Akihabara. Experience the neon-lit energy of Shinjuku at night.

Day 7: Leisure & Departure

Relax in a traditional onsen (hot spring), visit a local museum, or enjoy final shopping before departure.

Dubai - 5 Days

United Arab Emirates, Middle East

📅 5 Days | $1,100 - $1,700

View Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Burj Khalifa

Arrive in Dubai. Check into hotel and visit the iconic Burj Khalifa. Enjoy the sunset from the observation deck.

Day 2: Desert Safari & Dune Bashing

Experience thrilling dune bashing in the desert. Watch the sunset, enjoy a BBQ dinner, and camel riding under the stars.

Day 3: Dubai Mall & Palm Jumeirah

Shop at the massive Dubai Mall. Visit the Palm Jumeirah island by monorail and relax at the beach.

Day 4: Gold Souk & Dhow Cruise

Explore the traditional Gold Souk and spice markets. Evening dhow cruise on Dubai Creek with dinner.

Day 5: Beach & Relaxation

Spend the day at Jumeirah Beach. Relax at beach clubs, enjoy water sports, or visit the Dubai Fountain show.
